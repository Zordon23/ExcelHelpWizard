Insert a Chart:

  • Step 1:

    Select the cells you want to chart, including the column titles and row labels. These cells will be the source data for the chart. In our example, we will select cells A1:F6

  • Step 2:

    From the Insert tab, click the desired Chart command. In our example, we will select a Column chart.

  • Step 3:

    Choose the desired chart type from the drop-down menu.

  • Step 4:

    The selected chart will be inserted in the worksheet
